Pork Belly Recipe

Delicious Braised Pork Belly recipe for a perfect meal.

This pork belly recipe is a traditional Chinese recipe that is great for a family meal or a special occasion. The pork belly is braised in a flavorful sauce that is perfect for serving over rice or noodles.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound pork belly
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup dark soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup Shaoxing wine
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 cups water
  • 3 slices ginger
  • 3 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 2 scallions, cut into 2-inch pieces

Instructions:

  1. Cut the pork belly into 1-inch pieces.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium heat. Add the pork belly and cook until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the soy sauce, dark soy sauce, Shaoxing wine, sugar, water, ginger, garlic, and scallions to the wok. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer, covered, for 1 1/2 hours.
  4. Uncover the wok and increase heat to medium. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the sauce has thickened, about 30 minutes.
  5. Serve the pork belly over rice or noodles.
